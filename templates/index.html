NINEBARK

A Napa Valley dining experience by 2 Michelin star chef Matthew Lightner and AvroKO
featuring California-focused, market-forward cuisine.

The team

Chef Mattew Lightner

Over the last decade, Lightner has become a
name synonymous with culinary excellence.

Born and raised in the Midwest, Lightner first moved to the West Coast 1999, where he graduated culinary school and began his cooking career working in kitchens from Oregon to California including chef de cuisine at L'Auberge in Del Mat, California.

From there, Lightner set path overseas where he worked in some of the world's greatest kitchens including a stage at Noma in Copenhagen and an exclusive 18-month program at Mugaritz in the Basque region of Spain where he developed and perfected the techniques and flavors he is incorporating into the NINEBARK repertoire.

press

events

dinner

We offer various menu options for groups of 10 or more. All the menus are served family style. Please contact our events manager for more information

cocktails & canapes

Host a cocktail party from 10 to 200 guests at NINEBARK or Fagiani's Bar at NINEBARK, featuring a selection of our canapes is available to either be stationed or passed by the The NINEBARK staff.

wedding & ceremonies

Reserve our unique space for a wonderful, one-of-a-kind wedding backdrop. We can accomodate a variety of spatial and seating options for a ceremony, reception, sit-down dinner, dancing, and more. We welcome outside event planners, florists, and are flexible to meet your wedding needs.

brunch

We serve brunch 6 days a week from Tuesday-Sunday between 11am and 2.30pm, and we can accommodate private or semi-private brunch events at these times. We offer a seasonal 3-course prix fixe option for $35/person.

photoshoots and filming

NINEBARK' architecture, interiors and lighting provide a rich and textured setting for a photo shoot or filming.
